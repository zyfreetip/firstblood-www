Front-end Engineer

Precise to every pixel.

We are hiring a Front End Engineer to help us build the best gaming platform for Esports fans and players. You will be working with direct data streams to engineer a performance efficient app compliant with multiple browsers to provide gamers with an unprecedented experience.

We want our product to be user centered. We appreciate engineers who express themselves deeply and speak clearly. We need someone who has mastered modern Javascript frameworks and tools and can comfortably leverage developing technologies. It excites you to build things from scratch and know how to find the perfect balance of development speed and quality. You understand that this is a team sport and that we can only win together.

If you are passionate about gaming, Esports and working with the latest technologies, you will have a blast working at FirstBlood.


You will be:

- Developing the front-end portion of our app with our CTO and Product Manager.
- Working with our core leadership to select the best tools to solve complex problems.
- Building complex layouts with HTML and CSS.
- Using Angular 2 and other javascript frameworks to get the job done.
- Communicating with the design team and product management to make sure everything is implemented flawlessly.
- Balancing speed and quality expertly.


We’d love to see:

- 3+ years experience with Javascript.
- Experience building with Angular.
- You worked with Git before and know how to do it.
- Experience with other Javascript frameworks (such as React.js)
- Excellent communication skills (English is a must).
- You adapt fast and learn quickly.
- Design and user experience talent, with a user centric approach.
- Experience working with APIs.


Bonus points:

- You rock other js frameworks.
- Google Analytics is something you are familiar with.
- Experience with databases (SQL preferred).
- You're a passionate gamer.
- You know a thing or two about the blockchain.
- You worked on startups/projects before.


You get:

- A chance to work with one of the hottest companies in blockchain space
- To work remotely from home few days a week (spend that saved commuting time however you like).
- A competitive salary with benefits and perks.
- A chance to co-own the company by earning equity.


Don’t forget to include these in your application:

- Link to Github or Stackoverflow if you have them
- Links to projects
- CV or LinkedIn
- Tell us what excites you the most about this role in few sentences

Send us an email to: careers@firstblood.io
We are looking forward to hear from you!
