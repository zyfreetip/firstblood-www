  • Platform

  • Is it free to create an account?

    Yes! 100% free. We will not charge you any fees to open up an account.

  • What happens if a game's API malfunctions during a match?

    If the participants report different game outcomes, then the match will automatically go to the Jury Voting Pool.

  • What are Witnesses? Why use Witness nodes to verify game results?

    Witness nodes are the curators of matches. They serve as the final gateway before matches and rewards are processed. The network of Witness nodes act as a decentralized proxy that has the ability to connect to game APIs and cross-reference game outcomes to ensure no false, fraudulent, or suspicious matches are being processed by the platform.

  • Will there be a system to keep track of how often someone is reported for cheating?

    The smart contract itself will record information about past matches and jury results. We will aggregate this information along with additional information to create a reputation system. The reputation system will be part of the matchmaking process.

  • Can I compete in a match for free? Do I need to own 1SŦ?

    FirstBlood will host free-to-play tournaments for those who want to compete without risking anything. Entry will be completely free and prizes will range from merchandise to 1SŦ.

  • What cryptocoin will I be able to use on FirstBlood?

    Initially, you will be able to use 1SŦ and Ether (ETH) on our platform. Later versions of FirstBlood will likely expand to include other cryptocoins and payment methods.

  • Will we be able to put money on other players' games?

    No. FirstBlood is a skill-based gaming platform that only allows you to stake your own skill during a match.

  • How long until rollout after the crowdsale?

    We plan to launch an alpha version in December 2016, a private beta in March 2017, and a public beta in May 2017.

  • Will there be tournaments? Will any of them be free tournaments?

    Yes and yes! Verifiedusers will be able to organize their own events at any time! There will also be official pay-to-enter tournaments organized by FirstBlood. 100% free tournaments will be available if you want a chance to win rewards for free.

  • What languages will the platform support?

    We currently support English and Mandarin Chinese but will expand to include other languages. Korean, Russian, and Vietnamese are coming soon.

  • Legal

  • Are you guys compliant? How do we know you're legit?

    Our team consists of two ACAMS certified anti-money laundering/counter terrorist financing specialists. From the beginning, our team has included a great lawyer to keep us focused on staying out of trouble and help us navigate the regulatory landscape. Our goal is to create a safe, professional platform in order to bring a legitimate face to skill-based eSports gaming.

  • Do you have an AML/KYC policy?

    Yes we do. Our team consists of two ACAMS certified anti-money laundering/counter terrorist financing specialists. FirstBlood will implement a multi-tier policy to combat money laundering and will report suspicious activities to local financial intelligence units such as FINCEN.

  • How will you prevent underage gamers from using the platform?

    Our user verification process will include an age verification component to satisfy jurisdictions with minimum age requirements.

  • What region are you guys providing services to?

    Initially, we will cover the portion of the U.S. market that is receptive to skill-based fantasy sports operators.Later we will expand to include other locations around the world!We only plan to expand into areas where we are comfortable that our product can be used without clear legal violations.
