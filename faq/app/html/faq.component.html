  • Crowdsale & Token

  • What is FirstBlood?
  • What can you do with 1SŦ?

    • 1.Play matches using on-platform token

    • 2.Witness matches and vote in jury

    • 3.Host tournaments

    • 4.Claim rewards from activity

  • Is initial token allocation capped at a certain number?

    Currently there is no hard cap for the initial token allocation. At the end of the crowdsale, token issuance will be closed permanently.

  • How many 1SŦ will be created?

    There is no hard cap on the number of 1SŦ that can be created during the crowdsale. Token creation will be closed immediately and permanently after the crowdsale.

  • How can I purchase the tokens?

    During our crowdsale you can directly convert Ether into tokens through a transaction to the smart contract. We will provide a simple app that you can use through your web browser, Mist Browser, or MetaMask, to send the transaction to the smart contract. We are also working on partnerships with exchanges that will support our crowdsale.

  • Is there a pre-allocation of coins to the founders?

    Yes. A 10% allocation of 1SŦ will be allocated to the founders. This allocation will take place one year after the initial crowd-sale to ensure all founders are fully invested in the project’s success.

  • Is there a bounty program?

    Yes. 2,500,000 1SŦ will be pre-allocated to a FirstBlood bounty fund. The bounty fund will be used for pre-sale marketing and bug bounties. In addition, a 5% allocation of 1SŦ will be set aside for an eSports ecosystem fund to be used for promotions and eSports tournament rewards.

  • What are the benefits of holding a token?

    The main benefit of holding a token is that you can earn a percentage of the fees from any match when you participate as a Witness or in the Jury Voting Pool.

  • Do I need Ether to participate? What about Bitcoin, USD, Steem, etc.?

    In order to participate in the token sale, you need to send Ether to the token sale smart contract. We will integrate a third party payment processor to allow people who don’t have Ether to convert other forms of payment like Bitcoin, Steem, and USD.

  • Platform

  • Is it free to create an account?

    Yes! 100% free. We will not charge you any fees to open up an account.

  • If a game’s API malfunctions or goes down during a match, will the match automatically be contested and go to a jury?

    Yes. If either the Witnesses are unable to verify the game result through the API, or the participants report different game outcomes, then the match will automatically go to the Jury Voting Pool. If one player reports a loss and the other reports a win and the reports match the game result, the prize will be paid out to the rightful winner. If there is an inconsistency or dispute, Witnesses will not be able to verify the outcome and the dispute will move to the Jury Voting Portal.

  • What are Witnesses? Why use Witness nodes to verify game results?

    Witness nodes are the curators of matches. They serve as the final gateway before matches and rewards are processed. The network of Witness nodes act as a decentralized proxy that has the ability to connect to game APIs and cross-reference game outcomes to ensure no false, fraudulent, or suspicious matches are being processed by the platform.

  • Will there be a system to keep track of how often someone is reported for cheating?

    The smart contract itself will record information about past matches and jury results. We will aggregate this information along with additional information to create a reputation system. The reputation system will be part of the matchmaking process.

  • Can I compete in a match without putting up a stake? Do I need to own 1SŦ?

    FirstBlood will host free-to-play tournaments for those who do not want to put up a stake but still want to compete. Entry will be completely free and prizes will range from merchandise to 1SŦ.

  • What cryptocoin will I be able to use on FirstBlood?

    Initially, you will be able to use 1SŦ on our platform. Later versions of FirstBlood will expand to include other payment methods.

  • Will we be able to bet on other players’ games?

    No. FirstBlood is a skill-based wagering platform that only allows you to bet on yourself during a match. However, feel free to organize tournaments with your friends once we enable that feature!

  • How long until rollout after the crowdsale?

    Alpha testing of our platform has already begun for crowdsale participants! The first title we are supporting is Dota 2. We will allow more fans onto our platform as we refine and test the FirstBlood. You can expect our beta to launch in the Summer of 2017.

  • What about tournaments? Will there be free tournaments?

    Yes and yes! Vetted users will be able to organize their own events at any time! There will also be official pay-to-enter tournaments organized by FirstBlood. 100% free tournaments will be available if you want to win rewards without wagering.

  • What languages will the platform support?

    We currently support English and Mandarin Chinese but will expand to include other languages for our global markets.

  • Legal

  • Are you guys compliant? How do we know you’re legit?

    Our team consists of two ACAMS certified anti-money laundering/counter terrorist financing specialists. Additionally, we have an in-house attorney to make sure our operations are 100% compliant with local laws. Our seasoned lawyer has years of experience in the startup space, corporate environments, and worked for the First Circuit Court of Appeals for the U.S. Supreme Court. Our goal is to create a safe, professional, and compliant platform in order to bring a legitimate face to skill-based eSports wagering.

  • Do you have an AML/KYC policy?

    Yes we do. Our team consists of two ACAMS certified anti-money laundering/counter terrorist financing specialists. FirstBlood will implement a multi-tier policy to combat money laundering and will report suspicious activities to local financial intelligence units such as FINCEN.

  • How will you prevent underage gamers from using the platform?

    Our user verification process will include an age verification component to satisfy jurisdictions with minimum age requirements.

  • What region are you guys providing services to?

    Initially, we will cover all of the U.S. market with the exception of several states. Later we will expand to include other locations around the world! We will remain compliant with all local laws during our expansion.
